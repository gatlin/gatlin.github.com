Projects

Not everything, just the public domain stuff.

HNN

The Haskell Neural Network library. Under construction! The goal is to produce the fastest neural net library this side of the Mississippi. In particular we will place focus on parallelization.

Saturn

A concurrent network SAT solver written in Go. Pretty simple at the moment. Future plans involve parallelizing SAT instances to take advantage of all those pretty CPU cores you got there.

go-orc

A distributed computing and concurrency calculus, based on work from UT Austin, implemented in Go. Deceptively simple but potentially quite powerful. Written to eventually be used in Saturn.

Haskell SAT solver

A SAT solving algorithm written in Haskell. This was my attempt at understanding DPLL. I'd like to extend this with clause learning and backjumping, optimize the hell out of it, and put it on Hackage. Hacker News liked it?

pass

A Perl wrapper around the clingo answer set solver. Could be used in apps that want advanced logic programming support. It's a blunt instrument but it's useful.
