Join us!

The application is used to let us know that you are interested in cooperative living, and to let each house you are applying for know more about you.

After you submit your application, contact the house membership coordinator to schedule a visit to the house, if you haven't done so already. You are encouraged to attend dinner at any house you are interested in! Attending dinner is the best way to get a feel for the place.

At College Houses Cooperatives you will be a member of a community, not merely a resident. After acceptance, you will have the opportunity to express your ideas and will have the right to vote on house affairs. Your willingness to accept this responsibility and become part of our community is a major factor in your acceptance at College Houses.
